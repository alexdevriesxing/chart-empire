# Fictional Content Guidelines

- Never use real artists, bands, labels, publications, radio stations, streaming platforms, playlists, festivals, venues, awards, influencers, or charts in simulation content.
- Xing Records and Indie Music Promotion may appear only in clearly separated real-world partner/editorial areas.
- Do not imply endorsements, chart positions, reviews, airplay, or campaign results for real people.
- Do not imitate a recognizable living artist’s name, likeness, biography, voice, or catalog.
- Generated text must remain fictional and should use deterministic fallback templates if Workers AI is unavailable.
- Avoid hateful, sexualized-minor, defamatory, extremist, and rights-infringing generated content.
- Uploaded logos require ownership/permission checks and moderation before public display.
- New data should be tested against a denylist of protected or real-world names relevant to the content set.
